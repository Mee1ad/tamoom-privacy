Privacy Policy Last updated: [Date] This privacy policy explains how Soheil Ravasani ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the tamoom mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App. 1. Overview tamoom is an offline to‑do and task management application. The App stores your data locally on your device. At this time, the App does not use online accounts, cloud synchronization, analytics, advertising, or third‑party crash reporting services. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Information you provide - Task data: to‑dos, projects, areas, sections, tags, notes, due dates, and other task‑related information you enter into the App. - Backup files: when you choose to export a backup, the App creates a JSON backup file that contains your task data so that you can save it or move it between devices. All such information is stored locally on your device or in backup files that you manage. 2.2 Information collected automatically The App itself does not send any information to our servers, because we do not operate any backend for this App. The App may internally store technical information such as: - Local database records required to operate the App. - Timestamps for when backups are created. - Basic application metadata (such as the app name and version) used only to organize backup folders and files on your device. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us. 3. How We Use Your Information We use the information stored by the App solely for the following purposes: - To provide the core functionality of the App, including creating, editing, and managing your tasks, projects, and related data. - To create and restore backups when you explicitly choose to export or import data. - To improve reliability and user experience locally on your device, for example by remembering your preferences. We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties for marketing or advertising. 4. Data Storage and Backups - All task data is stored locally on your device using a local database and preferences storage. - When you choose to export a backup, the App writes a JSON file to a folder on your device (for example, your Downloads folder or another directory you select). You are responsible for managing, copying, or deleting these backup files. - When you import from a backup, existing data in the App may be replaced with the contents of the backup file you select. If you uninstall the App, locally stored data and settings may be removed from your device. Backup files that you have exported to shared storage or other locations will not be automatically deleted. 5. Permissions and Device Access To provide its functions, the App may request certain permissions, including: - Storage / file access: to read and write backup files in folders you choose (for example, Downloads/tamoom/backup) and to browse for backup files to restore. - Access to application information: to read the app name and version from the system to label backup folders and files. - Clipboard access: when you open the link field in a task, the App may check your clipboard to suggest recently copied text as a possible link. Clipboard contents are only used locally and are not stored or transmitted to any server. - Opening links in a browser: when you tap a link, the App uses the system’s browser or another app (via the operating system) to open that URL. The App does not track which links you open. Permissions are only used for the specific features described above. 6. Third‑Party Services The current version of tamoom does not integrate with third‑party services that collect user data such as analytics platforms, advertising networks, or remote crash reporting tools. The App uses open‑source and third‑party libraries (for example, for user interface components, local database access, file picking, and opening URLs), but these libraries operate within the App on your device and do not send your task data to us. If we add any third‑party services that process personal data in the future (such as analytics or crash reporting), we will update this privacy policy accordingly. 7. Children’s Privacy The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because the App does not require an account or online registration, we do not have a way to identify users by age. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information to us through the App, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action. 8. Your Control and Choices Because your data is stored locally: - You can view, edit, or delete your tasks and related data at any time from within the App. - You can delete backup files that you have exported by removing them from your device’s storage. - You can uninstall the App to remove its local data from your device (subject to your operating system’s behavior). If you back up your device or sync it with other services, copies of the App’s data may be included in those backups according to the settings of those services. 9. Data Security We take reasonable steps in the App’s design to avoid unnecessary transmission of personal data. However: - No mobile application or storage method is completely secure. - The security of your device, operating system, and any backups you create (including cloud backups managed by your device vendor) is outside our control. We recommend that you protect your device with a strong password or biometric lock and keep your operating system up to date. 10. International Data Transfers We do not operate servers for this App and do not intentionally transfer your personal data across borders. Any transfers of data may occur only as a result of your own actions, such as saving backup files to third‑party cloud storage services that you choose to use. 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes means you accept the updated policy. 12.
